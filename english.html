About the firm

Levin Fisher is a boutique law firm specializing in complex litigation (civil, commercial, corporate and white-collar criminal law) and in commercial law, corporate law, securities and capital markets.

The firm has earned a reputation among its clients in Israel and abroad, as well as in the legal community, as a leading boutique firm that takes an in-depth and creative approach to its representation of clients, handling all cases with the highest level of legal expertise.

The founding partners, Adv. Ido Levin (who manages the litigation practice) and Adv. Zvi Fisher (who manages the commercial-corporate practice), bring with them rich international backgrounds, and both have work experience in the USA.
